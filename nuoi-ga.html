Chuong Trai

Nuoi nen (ga thit)

  • Mat do: 8-10 con/m² (ga cong nghiep), 6-8 con/m² (ga ta)
  • Chat lot: tro trau, mun cua, day 8-10 cm
  • Mang an: 3-4 cm/con (mang dai) hoac 1 mang tron/25 con
  • Mang uong: 1 nuppon/10-12 con hoac mang dai 1.5 cm/con

Nuoi long (ga de)

  • Mat do: 5-6 con/m²
  • O de: 1 o/4-5 con, dat noi yên tinh, toi
  • Cau dau: 15-18 cm/con, cao 50-60 cm
