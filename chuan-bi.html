Yeu Cau Ve Chuong Trai

Vi tri

  • Cach xa khu dan cu toi thieu 500m, cuoi huong gio
  • Dia hinh cao rao, khong ngap ung, de thoat nuoc
  • Giao thong thuan tien de van chuyen thuc an, san pham
  • Gan nguon nuoc sach, xa nguon o nhiem

Thiet ke chuong

  • Huong chuong: Dong - Tay (truc dai theo huong Dong - Tay)
  • Mai chuong cao toi thieu 3m, co he thong thong gio
  • Nen chuong: Xi mang hoac gach, do nghieng 2-3% de thoat nuoc
  • Co he thong mang an, mang uong tu dong hoac ban tu dong
  • Ranh thoat nuoc thai xung quanh chuong

He Thong Xu Ly Chat Thai

  • Biogas: Ham u khi sinh hoc, phu hop trang trai vua va lon
  • U phan compost: U phan huu co tu chat thai ran, thoi gian 2-3 thang
  • Ho sinh hoc: Xu ly nuoc thai qua he thong ho lang, ho sinh hoc
  • Dem lot sinh hoc: Su dung che pham vi sinh xu ly chat thai tai cho
Luu y: Chuong trai phai duoc ve sinh, phun thuoc khu trung toan bo va de trong it nhat 7 ngay truoc khi nhap dan moi.
