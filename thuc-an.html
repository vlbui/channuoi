Nguyen Tac Dinh Duong

  • Dam bao du 5 nhom chat: dam (protein), beo (lipid), tinh bot (glucid), vitamin, khoang chat
  • Thuc an phai tuoi, khong bi moc, khong on, khong nhiem doc to (aflatoxin)
  • Cung cap nuoc sach day du - luong nuoc gap 2-3 lan luong thuc an kho
  • Cho an dung gio, dung luong, phu hop tung giai doan phat trien
  • Bao quan thuc an noi kho rao, thoang mat, tranh am moc
