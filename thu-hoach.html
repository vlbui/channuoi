Yeu Cau Truoc Khi Xuat Ban

  1. Ngung thuoc: Ngung su dung khang sinh theo thoi gian quy dinh (thuong 7-14 ngay tuy loai thuoc)
  2. Kiem tra suc khoe: Vat nuoi phai khoe manh, khong co trieu chung benh
  3. Giay kiem dich: Lien he tram thu y de duoc kiem dich va cap giay truoc khi van chuyen
  4. Nhat an: Ngung cho an 8-12 gio truoc khi xuat (giam hao hut, dam bao ve sinh)
Luu y: Theo Luat Thu y, tat ca vat nuoi truoc khi van chuyen, giet mo phai co Giay chung nhan kiem dich dong vat do co quan thu y cap.

Van Chuyen

  • Su dung phuong tien chuyen dung, sach se, co thong gio
  • Khong nhoi nhet qua dong, dam bao phuc loi dong vat
  • Van chuyen vao sang som hoac chieu mat, tranh nang nong
  • Co nuoc uong cho vat nuoi khi van chuyen duong dai
  • Mang theo giay kiem dich, ho so nguon goc

Truy Xuat Nguon Goc

He thong truy xuat nguon goc giup dam bao an toan thuc pham va tang gia tri san pham:

  • So ghi chep: Tu ngay nhap giong den ngay xuat ban
  • Thong tin truy xuat: Nguon giong, thuc an su dung, thuoc/vaccine da dung, ngay xuat, noi ban
  • Ma truy xuat: Dang ky ma so trang trai voi co quan thu y dia phuong
  • Chung nhan: VietGAP, GlobalGAP (neu du dieu kien) giup tang gia tri san pham
Goi y: Trang trai dat chuan VietGAP co the ban san pham vao sieu thi, chuoi cua hang thuc pham sach voi gia cao hon 15-30%.
