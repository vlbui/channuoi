Giong De Pho Bien

Giong Loai hinh Dac diem Trong luong truong thanh
Bach Thao Sua + thit Giong pho bien nhat VN, sua nhieu, de nuoi Duc 50-60 kg, cai 35-45 kg
Boer Thit Giong thit noi tieng, tang truong nhanh, co bap Duc 90-130 kg, cai 60-90 kg
Saanen Sua San luong sua cao nhat, long trang Duc 70-90 kg, cai 50-65 kg
De Co Thit Giong ban dia, nho con, ben benh, de nuoi Duc 30-35 kg, cai 20-25 kg
Lai Boer Thit Lai giua Boer va de dia phuong, uu the lai Duc 60-80 kg, cai 40-55 kg

Chuong Trai

Dac diem chuong nuoi de

  • San nang (san cao): San cach mat dat 50-80 cm, bang go hoac tre, khe ho 1-1.5 cm de phan lot xuong
  • Dien tich: 1.5-2 m²/de truong thanh, 0.5 m²/de con
  • Thong thoang: De can chuong kho rao, thong gio tot (de rat so am)
  • San choi: 3-5 m²/con, co bong mat, co cay bui de de leo
  • Mang an: Treo cao 40-50 cm, co nap de de khong dam len thuc an
Luu y: De rat so am uot. Chuong phai kho rao, khong dung vu nuoc. San nang la ly tuong nhat cho nuoi de.

Che Do Dinh Duong

Loai thuc an Luong/con/ngay Ghi chu
Co tuoi (co voi, co guinea) 3-5 kg Nguon xo chinh, phoi heo truoc khi cho an
La cay (so, mit, xoan, keo dau) 1-2 kg Bo sung vitamin, khoang tu nhien
Thuc an tinh (cam, ngo, kho dau) 0.2-0.5 kg Bo sung cho de mang thai, nuoi con, de duc phoi
Muoi khoang / Da liem Tu do Treo trong chuong
Nuoc sach 3-5 lit Tu do, nuoc sach

Luu y dinh duong

  • De la loai nhai lai, can nhieu chat xo tu co la
  • Khong cho an co uot (suong, mua) — de bi chuong bung day hoi
  • De cai mang thai thang cuoi: tang thuc an tinh len 0.3-0.5 kg/ngay
  • De con: tap an tu 2 tuan tuoi, cai sua luc 3 thang
