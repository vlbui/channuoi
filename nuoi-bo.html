Giong Bo Pho Bien

Giong Loai hinh Dac diem Trong luong truong thanh

Jersey Sua Sua beo, nho con, de nuoi 350-450 kg

Chuong Trai

  • Dien tich: 4-6 m²/con (bo thit), 8-10 m²/con (bo sua)
  • San chuong: Xi mang hoac gach, do nghieng 2-3%, co ranh thoat
  • Mai che: Cao toi thieu 3.5m, thong thoang, che nang mua
  • San choi: 10-15 m²/con, co bong mat
  • Mang an: Rong 60-80 cm, dai 70-80 cm/con
  • Nuoc uong: Mang uong tu dong hoac thung chua, 40-80 lit/con/ngay

Che Do Dinh Duong

Bo thit (vo beo)

Loai thuc an Luong/con/ngay Ghi chu
Co tuoi (co voi, co sả) 25-35 kg Nguon xo chinh, cho an tu do
Rom kho / Co kho 3-5 kg Bo sung khi thieu co tuoi
Thuc an tinh (cam hon hop) 2-4 kg Dam 14-16%, bo sung nang luong
Bo (bap cai, la mia) 5-10 kg Bo sung vitamin, khoang
Muoi khoang / Da liem Tu do Treo trong chuong de bo tu liem

Bo sua

  • Co tuoi chat luong cao: 35-50 kg/ngay
  • Thuc an tinh: 1 kg/2-3 lit sua san xuat (dam 16-18%)
  • Bo sung: premix khoang, canxi, phospho
  • Nuoc: 60-100 lit/con/ngay
