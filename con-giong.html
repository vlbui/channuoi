Tieu Chi Chon Giong

  • Nguon goc ro rang, co giay chung nhan kiem dich dong vat
  • Khoe manh: mat sang, nhanh nhen, long muot, da hong
  • Khong bieu hien benh tat: khong tieu chay, ho, tho kho, chay nuoc mui
  • Da duoc tiem phong vaccine day du theo lua tuoi
  • Co so giong uy tin, co giay phep kinh doanh giong
  • Phu hop voi dieu kien khi hau, muc dich nuoi (thit / trung / sinh san)

Cac Giong Ga Pho Bien

Giong Loai hinh Dac diem Thoi gian nuoi
Ga Ri Ga ta / thit Thit ngon, thom, gia ban cao 120-150 ngay
Luong Phuong Kiem dung De nuoi, lon nhanh, thich nghi tot 90-100 ngay
Ross 308 Ga cong nghiep Tang truong nhanh, ti le thit cao 40-45 ngay
Ga Dong Tao Ga dac san Chan to, thit dai, gia tri cao 150-180 ngay

Cac Giong Heo Pho Bien

Giong Dac diem Ti le nac Trong luong xuat
Yorkshire Than dai, tang truong nhanh 55-60% 90-110 kg
Landrace De nhieu con, ti le nac cao 58-62% 90-110 kg
Duroc Chat luong thit tot, lon nhanh 55-58% 100-120 kg
Heo Mong Cai Giong dia phuong, de nuoi, nhieu con 40-45% 60-80 kg

Cac Giong Bo Pho Bien

Giong Dac diem Trong luong truong thanh
Lai Sind Chiu nong tot, de cham soc, pho bien nhat 350-450 kg
Brahman Tang truong nhanh, thich nghi nhiet doi 500-700 kg
Bo Vang Viet Nam Giong ban dia, ben benh, de nuoi 200-300 kg
BBB (Blanc Bleu Belge) Sieu nac, ti le thit xay cao 700-1000 kg
Goi y: Nen mua giong tu co so co giay chung nhan va tham khao y kien can bo thu y dia phuong truoc khi nhap dan.
