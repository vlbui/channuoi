Goi y: Lon lai 3-4 mau (VD: Yorkshire x Landrace x Duroc) cho hieu qua kinh te cao nhat nho uu the lai.

Chuong Trai

Yeu cau thiet ke

  • Nen chuong: xi mang, do nghieng 3-5% ve phia ranh thoat
  • Dien tich: lon thit 1-1.2 m²/con, lon nai 4-6 m²/con
  • Nhiet do: 20-26°C (lon thit), 18-22°C (lon nai)
  • Chuong de: co o uum lon con, den suoi 32-35°C tuan dau
  • He thong lam mat: quat + tam nuoc khi nhiet do > 30°C

Nguyen lieu thuc an chinh

  • Nang luong: Ngo (bap), cam gao, khoai, san
  • Dam: Kho dau nanh, bot ca, kho dau lac
  • Bo sung: Premix vitamin-khoang, men tieu hoa, acid amin (Lysine, Methionine)
  • Nuoc: 2-3 lit/kg thuc an kho, nuoc sach tu do

Xuat Chuong

  • Thoi gian nuoi: 5-6 thang (lon thit cong nghiep)
  • Trong luong xuat: 90-110 kg, he so chuyen hoa thuc an (FCR) 2.5-3.0
  • Ngung thuoc: It nhat 7-14 ngay truoc xuat (tuy loai thuoc)
  • Nhat an: 8-12 gio truoc khi bat
  • Kiem dich: Lien he tram thu y cap giay truoc khi van chuyen
