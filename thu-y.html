Cac Benh Thuong Gap

Benh o Ga

Benh Trieu chung Phong / Tri
Newcastle Ho, tho kho, tieu chay xanh, liec dau Vaccine. Khong co thuoc dieu tri dac hieu
Gumboro Tieu chay trang, run ray, tu tu Vaccine phong. Dieu tri trieu chung + dien giai
CRD Kho tho, chay nuoc mui, mat sung Khang sinh: Tylosin, Tiamulin
Cau trung Tieu chay co mau, xanh xao Thuoc cau trung: Toltrazuril, Amprolium

Benh o Heo

Benh Trieu chung Phong / Tri
Dich ta heo Sot cao, xuat huyet da, tieu chay Vaccine. Khong co thuoc tri
LMLM Mung nuoc o mieng, mong, bau vu Vaccine FMD. Dieu tri trieu chung
Tai xanh (PRRS) Sot, ho, say thai, tai tim Vaccine. Nang cao an toan sinh hoc
Tieu chay (E.coli) Tieu chay vang/trang, mat nuoc Khang sinh + bu dien giai

Xu Ly Khi Co Dich Benh

  1. Cach ly ngay con vat benh ra khu rieng
  2. Bao co quan thu y dia phuong trong vong 24 gio
  3. Ngung nhap/xuat vat nuoi
  4. Phun khu trung toan bo chuong trai 2 lan/ngay
  5. Xu ly xac vat nuoi chet theo huong dan cua co quan thu y (chon, dot)
  6. Ghi chep day du dien bien dich benh
Canh bao: KHONG tu y dieu tri, mua thuoc khi chua co chan doan cua can bo thu y. KHONG ban, giet mo vat nuoi benh hoac nghi benh.
